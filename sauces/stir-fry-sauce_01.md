Stir-Fry Sauce
==============

A base for stir-fries

Ingredients
-----------

1/2 Cup

| Ingredient           | Amount  | Notes |
|:---------------------|:--------|:------|
| Oyster Sauce         | 1/4 cup |       |
| Soy sauce            | 2 tbsp  |       |
| Fish sauce           | 1 tbsp  |       |
| Thai seasoning sauce | 1 tbsp  |       |

Instructions
------------

1. Whisk together all ingredients
2. Store in fridge indefinitely
